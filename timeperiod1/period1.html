Tigers

Scientific name: Panthera tigris
Status: Endangered
Weight: Males up to 300 kg, females up to 170 kg
Size: Males up to 4 m long, females up to 3 m long
Population: Approximately 3,900
Habitat: Tropical, subtropical and temperate regions
Diet: Large prey including deer, wild boar and elephant calves
Range: Asia, including eastern Russia, northeastern China, India and Nepal
