Desktop angle

Follow the below instructions step by step:

  • Make sure there is less light from back of laptop.
  • If possibe face the camera with some light from the front(to get better focus)
  • Adjust the amgle to an acute one(60-70 degrees) between screen and laptop
  • Finally give permission to use your laptop front camera.



Phone angle

Open Marker in your mobile Gallery,then follow these steps.

  • Sit in an area where less sunlight hits your face(So that marker can be visible to laptop camera).
  • Now show your marker onto the screen. Try to place the phone on a flat surface like table and focus camera there.
